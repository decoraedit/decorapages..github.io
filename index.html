The fashion system is broken: from human exploitation to environmental issues. As we are all part of it whether as consumer, maker or creator we all have a voice and power to reinvent the flow of the industry.

Fashion is certainly an intriguing industry as the meaning of apparel is no longer a strict necessity but over time has become a strong medium for self-expression and cultural belonging. However, there's much more to it.

The Decora edit wants to be part of the fashion revolution raising awareness on the unsustainable practices of the industry and enchanting the innovations and contributions that are making fashion a better industry.

The Decora edit is a space for the real voices of fashion space that are actively making a difference within the industry, innovating practices and rethinking the system in a more sustainable way while bringing a real purpose to the system.

Whether is with the use of technology to bring transparency from the supply chain, smarter materials or better design thinking, The Decora edit wants to engage and open conversations on the breaking innovations that are moving the fashion industry towards an ethical ecosystem and possibly inspire more people to join the movement.
